5 % 2 = 1 because
Math.floor(5 / 2) = 2 (Quotient)
2 * 2 = 4
5 - 4 = 1 (Remainder)

Divide the numbers and remove the decimal (round down): this is the quotient.
Multiply the bottom operand against the quotient, then subtract this from the top operand.

6%3 = 0
Math.floor(6/3) = 2
3 * 2 = 6
6 - 6 = 0
